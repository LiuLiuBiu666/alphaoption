Developing a Daily Trading Routine

Developing a daily trading routine is critical to building habits that make you successful as a trader.

View risk disclosures

At Alpha Option, we believe that everything you have either done — or haven't done — can likely be traced back to a habit or routine. Let's make sure you've got great habits in place right now.

In this lesson we walk through a structured framework for preparing before the market opens, reviewing active positions during the session, and conducting an end-of-day (EOD) review so you improve week over week.

Welcome to this lesson on developing a daily trading routine. In this session we cover why a structured routine matters, what yours should look like before the market opens, how to stay disciplined during the session, and the end-of-day review process that separates consistent traders from the rest.

Remember — process creates consistency, and consistency creates results. Every successful trader has a repeatable system they follow every single day.
